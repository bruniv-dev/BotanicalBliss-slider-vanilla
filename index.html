SNAKE
The snake plant, or Sansevieria, is a hardy, low-maintenance houseplant known for its striking upright leaves and air-purifying qualities. It thrives in various lighting conditions and is perfect for beginners. With its sleek, architectural form, it adds elegance to any space.

BEEFSTEAK
The indoor beefsteak plant, or Perilla frutescens, is an herb with vibrant green to deep purple ruffled leaves. Popular in Asian cuisine for its minty-basil flavor, it thrives in sunny, well-drained spots indoors. Easy to grow in pots or containers, it adds color and texture to any indoor garden.

ZZ
The ZZ plant, or Zamioculcas zamiifolia, is a hardy, low-maintenance houseplant with glossy, dark green leaves. It's ideal for low light and infrequent watering, perfect for busy owners. Known for its air-purifying qualities, the ZZ plant brings natural beauty and improved air quality.

LACELEAF
Laceleaf plants are known for their delicate, lacy foliage, adding an elegant touch to indoor and outdoor spaces. They thrive in filtered light and require regular care to maintain their intricate appearance. Perfect for decorative use, they bring charm and beauty to any setting.
